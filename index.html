Our mission & principles

Applied Linguistics Press is committed to publishing research in an open access format, ensuring that it can be accessed and used by anyone, anywhere in the world. Through our dedication to promoting the advancement of knowledge in applied linguistics, we aim to contribute to the betterment of society as a whole.

ALP is run entirely by volunteers and guided by open science principles, fairness to all participants, and a genuine commitment to increasing the accessibility of applied linguistics to the global community.

Learn more about ALP

Diamond open access

No fees for authors. No fees for readers. A 100% diamond open access platform.

Rigorous peer review

Two-stage, single-blind review. Same rigour as traditional publishers — without the conflict of interest.

Any length, any format

Books, edited collections, multimedia projects, syllabi, video series — no page limits.

Author retains copyright

Works published under Creative Commons license. Others may share with citation — not sell.

Have an idea for a book? ALP wants to work with you.

We publish stand-alone titles as well as titles within our book series. If you have an idea for a book — no matter how long or short — feel free to reach out to the ALP General Editor, Luke Plonsky, to discuss what you have in mind.
