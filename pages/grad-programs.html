Graduate Programs in Applied Linguistics

A directory of graduate-level programs in applied linguistics, to help prospective students find PhD/EdD programs that match their scholarly interests and career goals.

Many applied linguistics programs are hard to find through online searches because they go by different names and are housed in many different types of departments and colleges. This directory seeks to make them easier to find. As of now, only US-based programs are included, but the goal is to add programs from other countries in the future.

For more information on finding, applying to, and getting funding for a PhD/EdD in applied linguistics, see the chapter by Ayşenur Sağdiç and Dan Isbell, which you can download here.

Note: Programs do not pay to be listed. Any doctoral program specializing in applied linguistics or related fields can be added — get in touch to add or update a listing.
