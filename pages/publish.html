Publish with ALP

Interested in publishing with Applied Linguistics Press? Here's what to do.

ALP currently publishes stand-alone titles as well as titles within individual book series. If you're interested in submitting a proposal to one of the series, check out their landing pages here and feel free to contact the series editors on next steps.

If you have an idea for a stand-alone book, feel free to reach out to the ALP General Editor, Luke Plonsky, to discuss what you have in mind. You can also submit a proposal directly to Luke via email using the template found here.

What we publish

ALP publishes books in digital/PDF format of any length on any topic within applied linguistics. Works can be single- or co-authored monographs or edited collections. The Press may also be open to other types of publications such as:

  • Popular linguistics writing
  • Video series and tutorials
  • Multimedia projects (videos, data, and text)
  • Journals in open access formats
  • Any other scholarly contribution to the field

As an exclusively digital press, our books are not limited to text and images. We encourage the inclusion of links, visuals, videos, data, and other media.

Submitting to a series

ALP currently publishes books in three distinct series:

  1. Syllabi in Applied Linguistics — Series Editors: Benjamin Kremmel & Paula Winke
  2. Affect and Care in English Language Education — Series Editors: Chunhong Liu, Bong-gi Sohn, & Angel Lin
  3. Corpus Linguistics and Technology-Mediated Language Education in the AI Era — Series Editors: Pascual Pérez-Paredes & Qing Ma

For series submissions, contact the relevant series editors directly. Their contact details are on the series pages.

Proposing a new series

Got an idea for a new book series? ALP would love to hear about it! Series proposals can address any area within the broad field of applied linguistics. You can download and fill out the series proposal template here, or get in touch with Luke via email to discuss before submitting.
